Types of Nouns

Let us recall “Nouns”.
From our previous knowledge we can say that: Nouns are naming words. All that exists has a name and all that is discovered and invented is also given a name. Nouns function to provide names to anything and everything under the sun. They are an important part of speech in all languages. They usually identify a person, a place, or a thing in particular. They also name things we cannot see or touch like happiness, sound, air, story etc. The endless list includes names of places, people, animals, things, concepts, qualities, organisations, communities, sensations, events etc.

Few examples of nouns are given below:
People: uncle, Henry, sister
Place: Japan, garden, school
Things: pen, television, bottle,
